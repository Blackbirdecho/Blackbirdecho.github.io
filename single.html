Best PS4 games to buy in July 2020

July 11, 2020 / by Khaled Ali Ahmed

Well, this month’s PS Plus games have not been a let down since we have got two good titles and an interactive movie kinda game. For those of you who didn't know, we got NBA 2K20, Rise of the Tomb Raider, and ERICA. Since I have played all these games I’m gonna tell what I enjoyed the most.

PS Plus July 2020

First up is NBA 2K20 which in my opinion is exactly like NBA 2K19. I didn't see any graphical improvements and the Career mode also seems to be the same. I would only play this game for the multiplayer aspect. So if you like basketball go for this one.

Second is the Rise of the Tomb Raider which I liked very much. This was my first Tomb Raider game and overall it was great. The storyline will keep you engaged and the gameplay is pretty smooth. When you download this game, you also get two expansion titles that can be played. The game’s playtime is almost under 14 hours. It also has co-op mode and a multiplayer mode. I would suggest you play this.

The third one is called ERICA, it’s an interactive movie you can say and I haven't completed it. So far it has not been boring. So if you’d like to try this, go for it.
