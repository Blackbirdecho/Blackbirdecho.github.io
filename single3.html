Is the Ps Plus Membership Worth it?

July 11, 2020 / by Games and Technology

PS Plus membership currently costs around 600rs per month and if you ask me, I’d suggest you subscribe for a year cause you get it at a cheaper price.

But first, the question is, Is it worth it??


Benefits of having PS Plus membership:

1. Online Multiplayer: For all those games you buy, most of them have a multiplayer feature where you can play with players all around the world. Casual or competitive, you can have a ton of fun!. Games have an option to create Clans or Squads and play with your friends on the same team. There is also coop gameplay for some, but to be able to access it we would need Ps Plus membership.

2. Free Games Every Month: The most amazing thing about this membership is that you get 2 free games each month for the ps4. These games are pretty good AAA titles and you can also expect some free DLC content for these games which you can download for free. For this month we got NBA 2k20, Tomb Raider and Erica(we got three because of the 10 year anniversary). 

3. Exclusive discounts: So the PlayStation store gives out discounts or sets up games for sale, but that's for everybody! for the Ps Plus members there are games with huge discounts sometimes and also in-game content which you can purchase for a very low price.

Sometimes members get access to a playable demo for a while to enjoy before other people get to play them. 

So these are the benefits of having a Ps Plus account. Hope you subscribe to it too!
