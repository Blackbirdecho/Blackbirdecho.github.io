Best Free Games to download in July

July 11, 2020 / by Khaled Ali Ahmed

So here is the list of games you can play for free on the ps4 this month.

Well, some of them are very old ones, to begin with, but the PlayStation Store doesn't giveaway games which have released a year or two back. The giveaways always are the games that have been released 3-4 years prior. Without any further ado, let's list them out.

1. Apex Legends:

So this game came out a long back, but for me, this is the best free fps game out there on the ps4 store. There are different characters with their unique abilities and to start off you get 3-4 characters and for the rest of them, you have to pay the in-game currency which you can earn by playing matches. The only mode you can play is the battle royale and you have to be in a squad of a total of three people including you. It has been rather difficult to win a match for me but this may not be the case for you.                                             

2. Call of Duty Warzone:

This one is relatively new. The graphics are kind of realistic and movement is slow. The weapon arsenal is quite big and a lot of customization options are also present. It is free but the only game mode is the battle royale. For the multiplayer mode, which you get for all call of duty games is not free and you will have to buy the game. Multiplayer mode consists of team deathmatch, solo deathmatch, capture the point, etc. There are many skilled players in this game so it'll be difficult to win.                                                                                                                                                                                                                       

Not a Free Game

3. Rocket League:

This one, by far is the most enjoyable game for me. The premise of this game is like that of a football league but with cars. You can use boosters and double jump across the stadium to score a goal with your teammates. It’s kind of easy when you get the hang of it. Unfortunately, It is not free. But I assure you that you will be glad you bought the game cause you won't get bored.                                                                                                                 

4. Destiny 2:

This is a great first-person shooter role-playing game. You can customize your character from the start, there are a lot of missions to be played alone, or along with friends or random players(coop) and there are also deathmatches if you want the gameplay to be challenging. The story mode was very engaging but after a certain level unless you buy the expansion pack you won't be able to level up more. So that's one thing I didn't like. Overall this game was fun and if you have friends that play this game then it's even better.

 So this was my list, feel free to download other games as well and play hard.
